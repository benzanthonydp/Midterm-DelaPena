Museum of Philippine Maritime History

Destrict. Aduana Building, Iloilo River Wharf, The Philippines, Muelle Loney St. Iloilo City Proper

The National Historical Commission of the Philippines Museum of Philippine Maritime History in Iloilo City is dedicated to showcasing the rich maritime heritage of the Western Visayas region. Situated in the Ground Floor of the Iloilo Customs House, the museum offers a comprehensive exploration of the region's seafaring history, spanning from ancient times to the present day. Its extensive collection of artifacts and exhibits includes maps, navigational instruments, ship models, and more, all illustrating the region's significant role in maritime trade and commerce. Through interactive exhibits and educational programs, the museum provides an ideal opportunity for visitors to delve into the region's maritime past and gain insight into the crucial role that the sea has played in shaping its cultural and historical heritage.
