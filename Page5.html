Lopez Heritage House

Nelly Garden, owned by the Lopez Clan, is one of the famous landmarks in Jaro

Nelly Garden, a cherished landmark in Jaro, Iloilo City owned by the Lopez Clan, is renowned for its expansive landscaped lawn and impressive American-colonial heritage house in art deco style. Managed by a travel agency, tours of the house can be arranged by visiting their office located within the compound. It's recommended to inform the management in advance for a smoother experience.

How to Get There

To reach Nelly Garden, head to E. Lopes Street in Jaro, Iloilo City. If you're commuting, taxis and jeepneys are readily available options. Simply hop on a Jaro CPU or Jaro Liko jeepney and ask the driver to drop you off at "Nelly Garden."
