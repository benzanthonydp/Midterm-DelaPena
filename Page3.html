Iloilo Business Park

Bonifacio Drive. City Proper 5000 Iloilo City, Philippines

A Thriving Township in Iloilo City

Megaworld's Iloilo Business Park embodies a captivating and visionary lifestyle, serving as a gateway to a robust synergy of progress, business, and leisure in Iloilo City and the broader Western Visayas region. Spanning 72 hectares, this master-planned mixed-use development in Mandurriao District offers a dynamic community project that is poised to become the vibrant township of the future, brimming with exciting possibilities.

Experience in Premiere Living

Megaworld's investment in the Iloilo Business Park, valued at P35 billion, represents its largest single venture outside Luzon. Within this vibrant township, one can marvel at the impressive array of structures, including BPO buildings comprising the region's largest IT-BPO Cyberpark, the iconic 3,700-seater Iloilo Convention Center, and luxury hotel brands such as Richmonde Hotel and Courtyard by Marriott. Additionally, the lifestyle shopping center Festive Walk Mall and VisMin's longest shop-and-dine strip, Festive Walk Parade, contribute to the dynamic atmosphere.

For those seeking residential opportunities, the condominium towers of One Madison Place, La Fayette Park Square, The Palladium, Saint Honore, and Saint Dominique offer exceptional options. Investors can also explore upcoming projects like The Pinnacle, a 20-storey pre-selling residential condominium, and the International Corporate Plaza, Iloilo City's modern pre-selling commercial condominium tower.
